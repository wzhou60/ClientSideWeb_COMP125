About Us

Our Story

McRonalds started as a small family-owned diner in 1985, with a simple mission: to serve high-quality, affordable meals in a welcoming environment. Over the years, we've grown into a community favorite, known for our delicious recipes and friendly service.

Our Mission

At McRonalds, our mission is to bring people together over great food. We believe in using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create meals that not only taste amazing but also support our local community.

Our Values

  • Quality: We prioritize quality in everything we do, from sourcing ingredients to customer service.
  • Community: Supporting our community is at the heart of our business.
  • Innovation: We constantly strive to create new and exciting recipes while keeping our classic favorites.

John McRonald

Founder and CEO of McRonalds
